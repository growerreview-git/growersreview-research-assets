# Common Failure Patterns — Skipped annual vent and fan servicing leading to premature motor failure and crop loss

Full GrowersReview guide: [Skipped Greenhouse Vent & Fan Servicing: What Growers Miss Before Motor Failure](https://growersreview.com/how-to-diagnose-and-prevent-skipped-vent-and-fan-servicing-leading-to-premature-motor-fail/)

## Failure Patterns

- Non-sealed motor bearings in any greenhouse application (rapid failure due to moisture/dust)
- Fixed-speed fans in greenhouses with fluctuating temperature/humidity demands
- Proprietary fan or vent components with limited or expensive replacement parts
- Lack of clear, manufacturer-provided maintenance schedule or service instructions
